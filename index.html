About Me

Bridget Shannon is originally from Jacksonville, Florida. She grew up along her four siblings playing soccer and singing in choir. Bridget later went on to study Music Education at Florida State University. She has been a Middle School Chorus Teacher for the past three years.

Bridget has performed in Carnegie Hall, Ireland, England, and Wales with her collegiate choir. She is passionate about music, community, human rights, and her family. She is excited for the career change from music teacher to web developer.

When Bridget is not singing or studying for UCF Coding Bootcamp, she enjoys spending time with her fiance, Jennifer, dog, Skye, and cat, Renegade.
